About Us

Welcome to our PCB manufacturing hub, where innovation meets precision. We are a passionate team of engineers, driven by a shared vision to revolutionize the world of custom PCB design and manufacturing. Our journey began with a simple idea—how could we create high-quality, affordable PCBs using cutting-edge technology? The answer led us to develop our very own CNC 2D plotter.

Our founders, Saimugil and Shravan ,set out to create a tool that would not only cater to their own project needs but also serve the broader engineering community. After countless hours of development, they successfully built a CNC 2D plotter that uses two NEMA 17 stepper motors with DRV8255 drivers and a servo for pen holding. This plotter precisely draws circuit designs on copper-clad boards, which are then processed into fully functional PCBs.
